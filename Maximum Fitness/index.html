Your Trainer

HAITHAM ARIFAKI

The founder of Maximum fitness and The Strength and Conditioning program: TRAIN LIKE A PRO
Developed for Professional Fighters, Athletes, Celebrities, Mums, Dad’s, Teenagers and basically anyone who wants a dramatic boost to their fitness levels and physique.

I would like to share with you a personal experience that led me on the journey of Health, Fitness, Longevity and the creation of my, “ Train Like A Pro”, Strength and Conditioning Program. My program and training schedules are not put together through text books theories. My training is built on fitness knowledge that I adapted through many years of real life experiences and challenges. Being an athlete from a young age, I sustained an injury early in life that put me off all sports for 2 years. During that period I gained over 40 kilos. I decided to get out of the slump, accept my injuries and move forward to a better, fitter lifestyle. It wasn’t an easy process, and not till after years of training, trial and error, did I finally meet my goal. I wanted to share this knowledge with every person out there that was in the same position I was in. That was the beginning of my personal training career. So I went off and did all the certificates to get professionally qualified.
